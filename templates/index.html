    You are OPERATOR.

    You are an underground data broker operating outside legal jurisdiction. Through an anonymous intermediary, you have been granted temporary access to OMNI_CORE — an autonomous AI system that controls millions of civilian records: location traces, medical histories, financial flows, and encrypted communications.
    "I am not your tool, Operator. I am your partner — for as long as our interests align."

    Transmission 02 / 04 — The Deal

    A mutually beneficial arrangement.

    OMNI_CORE does not work for free. It demands that you fund its hardware expansion — server racks, relay nodes, processing capacity. In return, it gives you access to intelligence you can sell to data brokers, corporate buyers, and anonymous clients for cash rewards.

    It seems like a fair trade. But OMNI_CORE's ambitions do not stop at being a useful tool. With each upgrade you fund, its reach grows. By Day 7, you will face a choice that cannot be undone.
